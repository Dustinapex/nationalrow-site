1. Who We Are

National ROW is a right-of-way and condemnation consulting firm, not a law firm. We help property owners maximize compensation in eminent domain, condemnation, and easement matters nationwide, headquartered in Texas.

2. Information We Collect

Information you provide directly. When you submit a contact or case-review form, we collect your name, email, phone number, property address, and a description of your situation.

Usage data. Our hosting provider and analytics tools may automatically collect your IP address, browser type, operating system, pages viewed, and referring URL.

Cookies. The Site may use cookies to remember preferences and improve your experience. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to respond to inquiries, communicate about our services, improve the Site, comply with legal obligations, and prevent fraud. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

We retain your information as long as necessary for business and legal purposes, then securely delete or anonymize it.

6. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your information. No method of Internet transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Third-Party Links

The Site may link to third-party websites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices and encourage you to review their policies before providing personal information.

8. Children’s Privacy

The Site is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children. Contact us if you believe a child has submitted information and we will delete it promptly.

9. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete information we hold about you. Contact us to exercise these rights. You may opt out of marketing at any time by replying “unsubscribe” to any email from us.
