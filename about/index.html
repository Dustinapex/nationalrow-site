About National ROW

Always on the Property Owner’s Side

An independent right-of-way consulting firm representing property owners across all 50 states. Never the agency, utility, or pipeline company — always you.

Who We Are

National ROW is an independent right-of-way consulting firm headquartered in Texas. Since the 1980s, we have represented landowners, businesses, and property owners who are facing eminent domain, condemnation, easement acquisitions, and all forms of right-of-way takings.

We are not a law firm, and we are not aligned with any government agency, pipeline company, utility, or transmission developer. Our only client is the property owner — the person whose land, livelihood, and investment are at stake when a taking occurs.

Our team has handled cases involving highway condemnation by state DOTs, pipeline easements for natural gas and oil transmission, electric transmission line easements, solar and wind energy facility easements, transit and rail takings, and more. If something crosses your land or takes your property, we have almost certainly handled a case like yours.

Why Property Owners Choose National ROW

Our consultants have spent decades building relationships with attorneys, appraisers, and right-of-way professionals across the country — giving you access to a full team of experts when you need them most.

Our Approach

When you contact National ROW, we begin with a free case review. We look at the offer you have received, the property being taken, and the applicable state law to give you an honest assessment of whether we believe we can recover more for you.

If we take your case, we negotiate directly with the acquiring agency or company on your behalf. We gather evidence of true fair market value, document severance damages to your remaining property, and pursue every avenue the law provides — including business loss, relocation costs, and diminution of access — to maximize your total compensation.

If your case needs to go further — to a condemnation hearing or a jury determination of just compensation — we don’t hand you off and walk away. We coordinate your legal team, make sure your attorney has everything they need to make the strongest possible case, and stay with you through to the final number. You have one point of contact throughout. That’s the whole point.

We operate on a contingency basis: if we do not recover more than the initial offer, you owe us nothing. That structure means our interests are always perfectly aligned with yours.
