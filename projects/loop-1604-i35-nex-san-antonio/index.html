What TxDOT's Offer May Miss

Urban and suburban parcels near San Antonio are among the most complex ROW cases in Texas. TxDOT's appraisers may overlook significant compensable items.

Commercial Property Diminution

Commercial parcels along Loop 1604 and I-35 can lose significant frontage value, parking, or access — all compensable under Texas law.

Business Interruption

If construction affects your ability to operate a business, certain economic losses may be recoverable depending on the circumstances.

Remainder Damage

When only part of your parcel is taken, the remaining portion may be worth less than before — you're entitled to compensation for that reduction.

Access & Ingress/Egress

Loss of direct highway access, driveway closures, or changes to traffic patterns can materially reduce property value.

Landscaping & Improvements

Fences, irrigation systems, retaining walls, and mature trees removed during construction have real dollar value.

Noise, Proximity & View

Elevated structures and increased traffic near remaining structures can affect marketability and value in ways TxDOT may not account for.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I negotiate TxDOT's offer on Loop 1604 or I-35 NEX?

Yes. TxDOT's offer is a starting point. You may present your own appraisal and negotiate for a higher amount. TxDOT must ultimately pay just compensation — as determined by a court or jury if necessary.

What if TxDOT already filed a condemnation suit?

You still have the right to contest the amount of compensation. The condemnation process has defined steps — special commissioners, a hearing, and if needed, a jury trial on value. Contact us immediately if you've been served.

Is National ROW a law firm?

No. National ROW is a right-of-way consulting firm, not a law firm. We do not provide legal advice. We help landowners understand their options, quantify all categories of damages, and negotiate compensation.

My property is commercial — do you handle those cases?

Yes. Commercial cases along Loop 1604 and I-35 are some of the highest-value ROW cases in Texas. We have experience with commercial valuation, access damages, and business-related impacts.
